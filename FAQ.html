KVRX FEST

May 10-11th
Austin, TX

FAQ

General Info

Q: When is the Fest?
A: KVRX Fest takes place on May 10th and 11th, 2019. Official door times and set times to come.

Q: Where is the Fest?
A: KVRX Fest will take place in Austin’s Red River District, between three stages at Cheer Up Charlie’s and Symphony Square. It’s just a quick walk to get back and forth between the venues- plus we’ll be livestreaming the Cheer Up stages at Symphony Square.

Q: Where should I look for updates and announcements about the Fest?
A: Keep an eye on our social media! Take your pick: Twitter, Facebook and Instagram.

Ticketing Info

Q: Ticket Cost?
A: $40! That’s ONE DOLLAR per band!

Q: Who can come to the Fest?
A: Anyone over the age of 18. (Sorry, kids)

Q: When is the last day to buy tickets?
A: Tickets are available until the last day of the fest, May 11th, or until they sell out.

Q: I bought my ticket, how can I pick up my wristband?
You can pick up your wristband at:
- Hearst Student Media Building (HSM), 3rd floor main office on the UT campus
- The Fest preview show, April 20th at Space 24 Twenty
- Will Call when you arrive at Cheer Up Charlie's
- Mail (for out of town customers ONLY, email info@kvrx.org for more info)

When picking up your wristband, please bring a valid ID and proof of purchase (a confirmation number or virtual Eventbrite ticket).

Q: VIP passes?
A: While we won’t have “VIP” tickets available, we'll be announcing a Local Live ticket package soon. And don’t worry, you'll be able to upgrade your GA ticket with no extra cost.

Day of Info

Q: What should I bring to the Fest?
A: As little as possible! There will not be any lockers available, so please only bring a small bag with the essentials: ID, wallet, phone and earplugs.

You will not be granted entry if you bring weapons of any kind, drugs, or alcohol. No large backpacks, hammocks, chairs or umbrellas either.

Q: How can I get to the Fest?
A: Parking in downtown Austin is tough. There is pay-to-park street parking in the surrounding area, but the best bet is to ride share or take the bus!

Q: Is there a festival map available?
A: Yes, coming soon.

Q: Set times?
A: We’ll release a schedule with set times and stage information in April.

Q: Food and drink?
A: No outside food or drink permitted is in the venues. We have a *delicious* announcement about food options coming soon.

Q: Re-entry policy?
A: Re-entry is allowed, as sets will be split between the venues. They are two blocks away, use the cross walks!

Q: What if it’s super crowded by the stage when my favorite band is playing?
A: As for any show, please get to the stage early if you want a good spot.

Q: Will there be phone charging stations?
A: No, so bring a portable phone charger.

Q: My question wasn’t on here, who can I ask?
A: Send any additional questions to info@kvrx.org
